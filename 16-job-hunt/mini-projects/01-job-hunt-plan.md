# Mini-project — Job Hunt Plan

## Goal

Produce a one-page job-hunt plan you could hand to a trusted friend for review.

## Deliverable

A concise plan covering target, pipeline, schedule, metrics, and runway.

## Required behavior

1. Include a one-paragraph target statement.
2. Name the pipeline shape and justify it.
3. Show a weekly schedule.
4. Define metric thresholds that trigger a pause and revise.
5. Include a runway estimate.

## Acceptance criteria

- One page, readable, and realistic.
- Includes an explicit rest plan.
- Uses numbers, not vague statements.
- A friend could review it in under 5 minutes.

## Hints

- Keep it practical and honest.
- If your numbers look bad, that is useful information.
- Treat the plan as a living artifact.

## Stretch goals

1. Add a bad-market contingency.
2. Add a networking outreach script.
3. Add a monthly review template.
