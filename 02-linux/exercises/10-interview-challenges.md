# Module 02 · Extended Assessment — Interview Challenges

## 1) Warm-up

Answer five fast shell/Linux troubleshooting prompts.

## 2) Standard

Complete two timed command-line challenges and explain your reasoning.

## 3) Bug hunt

Break one solution with edge cases and fix it.

## 4) Stretch

Explain your approach in plain language for a junior teammate.

## 5) Stretch++

Run a 25-minute mock and record one concrete improvement.

**Takeaway:** Interviews test reasoning and safety, not memorization.
