# Mini-project — 08-interviewing

_Companion chapter:_ [`08-interviewing.md`](../08-interviewing.md)

**Goal.** Build an interview-ready kit.

**Acceptance criteria.**

- 60-second intro pitch, rehearsed aloud, under 90 seconds reliably.
- 6 behavioral stories in STAR form, each under 2 minutes.
- A 3-minute version of your headline project talk with a drawn architecture diagram.
- 60+ solved coding problems logged, tagged by pattern.
- 3 completed mock interviews with notes.
- A go-to list of 5 questions you ask at the end of rounds.
