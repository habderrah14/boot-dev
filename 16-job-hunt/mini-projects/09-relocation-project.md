# Mini-project — 09-relocation

_Companion chapter:_ [`09-relocation.md`](../09-relocation.md)

**Goal.** Produce a relocation plan for one specific destination city.

**Acceptance criteria.**

- Named target city + rationale (career, life, finance).
- Chosen visa path with timeline and eligibility check.
- List of 20 confirmed sponsor companies for your nationality.
- Budget spreadsheet with cost categories and amounts (conservative).
- Exit plan paragraph (what triggers "go home" and what that costs).
