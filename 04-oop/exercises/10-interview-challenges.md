# Module 04 · Extended Assessment — Interview Challenges

## 1) Warm-up

Answer five concise OOP design questions.

## 2) Standard

Solve two timed class-design prompts with rationale.

## 3) Bug hunt

Break and fix one design under changing requirements.

## 4) Stretch

Explain your model to a junior engineer.

## 5) Stretch++

Run a 25-minute mock and capture one improvement.

**Takeaway:** OOP interviews evaluate design reasoning under constraints.
