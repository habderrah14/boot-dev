# Module 02 · Extended Assessment — System Design Prompt

## 1) Warm-up

Define filesystem hierarchy, process model, env config, and least privilege.

## 2) Standard

Design a deployment script flow with validation, logging, and rollback checkpoints.

## 3) Bug hunt

Identify three scale/operability risks in your design and propose mitigations.

## 4) Stretch

Write a short capacity memo for disk/IO/log growth assumptions.

## 5) Stretch++

Peer-critique the design and require one resilience upgrade.

**Takeaway:** Ops design is part of backend architecture.
