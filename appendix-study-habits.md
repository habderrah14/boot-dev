# Appendix — Study habits (12-month horizon)

> Backend competence is **months of small sessions**, not one heroic weekend. This appendix is the “operating system” for studying alongside [Boot.dev](https://www.boot.dev/) and this companion.

## Weekly rhythm (default)

| Block | Time | Focus |
|-------|------|--------|
| **Deep** | 3× 90 min | New chapter + exercises + one mini-project step |
| **Review** | 2× 45 min | Spaced repetition: last week’s quiz + 3 old exercises |
| **Rest** | 1 day | No guilt — memory consolidates offline |

Adjust to your life; do not zero out the rest day for “more grind.”

## Spaced repetition for DSA (Module 06)

- After you solve a pattern (e.g. two pointers), **re-solve it on day 2, day 7, day 21** without peeking.
- Keep a **one-line log** per problem: “forgot invariant X” — that line is your future flashcard.

## When to pause for projects

If you have not **shipped** anything in 4 weeks, steal one session to finish a vertical slice (API + DB + README + tests). Shipped beats “almost understood.”

## Anti-burnout guardrails

- **Stop rule:** if you reread the same paragraph 3 times without progress, switch to exercises or sleep — do not stare.
- **Comparison:** compare yourself only to **last-month you**.
- **Ask early:** paste minimal repro (see [Onboarding](appendix-onboarding.md)) after 30 minutes stuck, not after 6 hours.
