# Mini-project — 05-linkedin

_Companion chapter:_ [`05-linkedin.md`](../05-linkedin.md)

**Goal.** Rebuild your LinkedIn so a recruiter for your target role would send you a message after 20 seconds.

**Acceptance criteria.**

- Professional photo, branded-ish cover image.
- Headline follows the template and includes location + status.
- About is 3 paragraphs and ends with contact/DM line.
- Experience and project dates match your resume exactly.
- Featured section has 3–4 live, linked items.
- Skills: 15–25, top 3 pinned to match the target role.
- Open to Work configured honestly (recruiters-only if employed).
