# Mini-project — 01-strategy

_Companion chapter:_ [`01-strategy.md`](../01-strategy.md)

**Goal.** Produce a one-page job-hunt plan you'd hand a friend.

**Acceptance criteria.**

- Target statement (one paragraph).
- Pipeline shape (named) with rationale.
- Weekly schedule (hours and blocks).
- Metric thresholds that trigger a pause-and-fix (e.g., "if response rate < 5% after 30 apps, revise resume").
- Timeline estimate with cash-runway check.

**Hints.** Keep it to one page. A friend should be able to read it and tell you if your plan is realistic. If they can't understand the plan in 5 minutes, it's too vague.

**Stretch.** Add a "bad market" contingency section: what changes if the market tightens while you're hunting.
