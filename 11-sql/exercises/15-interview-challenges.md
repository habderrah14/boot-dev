# Module 11 · Extended Assessment — Interview Challenges

## 1) Warm-up — fast SQL round

Solve five quick prompts (SELECT, JOIN, GROUP BY, HAVING, window function).

## 2) Standard — two timed challenges

Complete two medium SQL interview problems with explanations and complexity/plan reasoning.

## 3) Bug hunt — edge-case attack

Break one solution with nulls, duplicates, and sparse data; then fix and explain.

## 4) Stretch — explain-your-query memo

Write how your query works in plain language for a non-SQL peer.

## 5) Stretch++ — mock interview loop

Run a 30-minute mock and document one improvement for your next round.

**Takeaway:** Interview SQL is reasoning + communication under constraints.
