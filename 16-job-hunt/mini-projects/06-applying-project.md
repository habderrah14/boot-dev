# Mini-project — 06-applying

_Companion chapter:_ [`06-applying.md`](../06-applying.md)

**Goal.** Apply to 30 roles across a 2-week window with consistent process and tracker.

**Acceptance criteria.**

- 30 tracker rows with all fields filled.
- Each submission completed in 15 minutes or less on average.
- Resume was tailored (not reused) for at least 20 of 30.
- Conversion numbers computed and interpreted at the end.
- At least one explicit *skip* decision recorded (prove you're filtering, not firehose-ing).
