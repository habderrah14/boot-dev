# Mini-project — 07-networking

_Companion chapter:_ [`07-networking.md`](../07-networking.md)

**Goal.** Start a weekly networking practice and run it for 4 consecutive weeks.

**Acceptance criteria.**

- Week-by-week log of actions: old contacts revisited, public interactions, cold messages, follow-ups.
- At least 3 real conversations (calls, in-person, or multi-message threads with substance).
- One specific, well-timed ask based on one of those conversations — accepted or declined.
- Reflection note: what worked, what felt awkward, what you'll keep doing.
