# Module 15 · Extended Assessment — Interview Challenges

## 1) Warm-up — quick scenarios

Answer five short pub/sub scenarios (routing, retries, duplicates, ordering, DLQ).

## 2) Standard — two design challenges

Solve two timed interview prompts on event flow and consumer resilience.

## 3) Bug hunt — break your design

Stress one design with duplicates, out-of-order events, and partial outages; then patch.

## 4) Stretch — tradeoff memo

Explain your delivery guarantee choice and what you intentionally gave up.

## 5) Stretch++ — mock interview

Run a 30-minute mock and write one concrete improvement for your next answer loop.

**Takeaway:** Messaging interviews test tradeoff clarity more than API trivia.
