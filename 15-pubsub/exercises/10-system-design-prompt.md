# Module 15 · Extended Assessment — System Design Prompt

## 1) Warm-up — architecture terms

Define at-least-once, at-most-once, exactly-once (practical), DLQ, outbox, and replay.

## 2) Standard — event-driven design

Design an event pipeline for one domain (orders, notifications, audit). Include topics/queues, routing keys, and failure semantics.

## 3) Bug hunt — scaling breakpoints

Identify three breakpoints at 10x traffic and propose mitigations.

## 4) Stretch — capacity memo

Estimate throughput budget and broker bottleneck with explicit assumptions.

## 5) Stretch++ — peer critique

Review peer design and require one resilience improvement before launch.

**Takeaway:** Message topology is a product decision with reliability consequences.
