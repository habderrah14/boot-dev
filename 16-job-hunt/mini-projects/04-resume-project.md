# Mini-project — 04-resume

_Companion chapter:_ [`04-resume.md`](../04-resume.md)

**Goal.** Produce a one-page base resume plus one tailored version.

**Acceptance criteria.**

- Base resume: one page, plain PDF, passes ATS paste-into-text-editor sanity check.
- All projects have: name, one-liner, 2–3 impact bullets, links.
- At least 5 bullets in "X using Y → Z" form with real numbers.
- Tailored copy for a specific real posting; diff from base is visible and defensible.
- Reviewed by at least one person who gives a "would forward" verdict.
