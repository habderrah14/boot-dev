# Module 04 · Extended Assessment — Debugging Incident Lab

## 1) Warm-up

Classify three OOP failure symptoms and first inspection point.

## 2) Standard

Write one postmortem for inheritance/encapsulation misuse.

## 3) Bug hunt

Fix three issues: leaky abstraction, wrong override behavior, hidden shared state.

## 4) Stretch

Draft a runbook for "unexpected polymorphic behavior in production".

## 5) Stretch++

Peer simulation and one runbook improvement.

**Takeaway:** OOP bugs are often contract and boundary bugs.
